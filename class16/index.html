Project Ideas

The Team and General Idea for your final project is due Thursday, 19 March (ready to present in class that day).

Your team should be prepared to present a few slides with:

  • Name of your project
  • List of your team members
  • Purpose of your project: how it satisfies the four project desiderata
  • What you plan to do
  • What you hope the outcome will be
